Upcoming:

We Are the Stories We Tell

with Stanley Hainsworth

September 25, 2013

7:00 pm

52 Underground
330 SE Martin Luther King Blvd.
Portland, OR 97214
Register

If the brands we choose and associate with speak to who we are as a person, doesn’t that make brands part of our story and personality? So, how do you craft love affairs between brands and individuals? Love that blossoms; love that lasts?

By starting at the genuine heart of a brand, with an approach that elevates story over style. Stories that evolve from a series of carefully crafted cumulative experiences that connect us via our shared affinities, desires and convictions. These stories manifest themselves as experiential tapestries, nurturing our sense of collective unity as they empower people to engage with one another, and the brands they love, in more resonant, more human ways.

About Stanley Hainsworth

Before founding the creative juggernaut known as Tether, Stanley was VP Global Creative of Starbucks where he oversaw all creative aspects of the brand – from new products, packaging systems and seasonal promotions to brand campaigns and advertising. Stanley also spent twelve years at Nike as a Creative Director working on everything from product launches to the Olympics. After Nike, he moved to Denmark to join the Lego Company as their Global Creative Director where he directed a total visual overhaul of the brand from top to bottom, including packaging, the web, retail and brand stores.

About Designspeaks:

Designspeaks exists to encourage people to talk about design. Our purpose aims to explore design within the multidisciplinary: architecture, brand, communications, experience, film, fashion, graphic, and industrial. To establish an interface within the context of real life. To support the most compelling regional voices in design. To talk it through.
